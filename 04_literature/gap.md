## Research Contribution

This research seeks to address the identified gaps by developing a Random Forest-based Machine Learning model for bankruptcy-related credit risk classification using financial indicators obtained from the Superintendence of Banking, Insurance and AFP (SBS) of Peru. The study contributes to the literature by:

* Developing and validating a Machine Learning model for bankruptcy-related credit risk estimation within the Peruvian financial sector.
* Evaluating the applicability and predictive performance of Random Forest using SBS financial indicators.
* Identifying the financial variables with the greatest influence on risk classification through feature importance analysis.
* Establishing a reproducible Machine Learning workflow documented through GitHub and transparent research practices.
* Expanding empirical evidence regarding Artificial Intelligence applications in financial risk management within emerging economies.
