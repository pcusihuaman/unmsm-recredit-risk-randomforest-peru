## Research Contribution

This research seeks to address the identified gaps by developing a reproducible Random Forest-based bankruptcy risk classification model using financial indicators obtained from the Superintendence of Banking, Insurance and AFP (SBS). The study contributes to the literature by:

1. Evaluating the applicability of Random Forest within the Peruvian financial sector.
2. Identifying the most influential financial indicators associated with bankruptcy-related risk.
3. Providing a reproducible machine learning workflow documented through GitHub.
4. Establishing a foundation for future benchmarking against alternative machine learning algorithms.
5. Expanding the evidence base regarding Artificial Intelligence applications in financial risk management within emerging economies.
